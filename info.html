Animetions

    • Anime

    Anime is a style of animation that originated in Japan and has become popular worldwide. It encompasses a wide range of genres, including action, romance, fantasy, science fiction, horror, and slice of life.

    • Manga

    Manga is a style of Japanese comic or graphic novel that features a wide range of genres and themes. It typically has a distinctive art style and is read from right to left, which is the traditional Japanese reading format.

    • Manhwa

    Manhwa refers to Korean comic books or graphic novels, similar to manga in Japan. It has its own unique style and storytelling conventions, often characterized by detailed art and a focus on character emotions.
